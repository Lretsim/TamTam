How to Play?

Welcome to the "Where's TamTam?" game! Ready for some fun? Your mission is simple:

  • Find Tamtam! Click the image of Tamtam within 30 seconds to score points.

  • Each time you find Tamtam, you'll earn:
    • +5 points to your score.
    • +5 seconds to your remaining time.
    An interesting Fun Fact will give you some information and then new random hideout for Tamtam will appear after you find TamTam. Keep an eye out for Tamtam's sneaky hiding spots!

  • If you click wrong, 2 seconds will be deducted from your timer. Be careful!
  • If time runs out before you find Tamtam, the game ends. But don't worry, you can always Start Game and try to beat your score!
